About Us

For over 50 years this iconic coffee lounge has treated the Hampstead population to great coffee, tea and… raisin toast! During the past several years, since the Villa Bianca Group took control of the establishment, we have extended the menu to offer breakfast, lunch & dinner.

Under the supervision of our executive chef, we created a great-tasting, freshly sourced, menu for all to enjoy. Coffee Cup has once again taken its rightful place as one of Hampstead’s most popular spots. Welcoming in a new generation of customer, who will one day tell their kids of the times when they used to visit The Coffee Cup Hampstead.
